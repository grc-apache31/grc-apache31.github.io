Introduction

Monet Photography

At Monet Photography, we strive to create memories through photography. We want to capture the raw spirit of the moments we share and turn them into something that can be enjoyed by everyone! Monet Photography practices the art of mindful photography and can't wait to help you experience it yourself.

Target audience

The target audience would be those who are interested in booking professional photography. This can be for a variety of different photoshoot scenarios, such as friendship photoshoots, headshots, senior pictures, and club shoots.

Their goal in using this site would be to find prices for photo shoots, contact Monet Photography, and get an idea about the type of photography that this brand offers.
